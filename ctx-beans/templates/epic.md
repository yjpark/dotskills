# Epic Template

An epic is a thematic container for related work. Epics are not worked on directly — they group child beans toward a common purpose.

## Sections

### Vision
The big picture. What does this epic achieve when complete?

### Goals
Specific, measurable outcomes this epic delivers. What will be true when it's done?

### Key Deliverables
Major pieces of work involved. These typically become child beans.

### Success Criteria
How to know the epic is complete. What signals that all the goals have been met?

### Scope & Boundaries
What is included in this epic. What is explicitly out of scope.
